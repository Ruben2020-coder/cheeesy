Cheesy Quesadillas

requirements- you need 4 flour tortillas (8 inches) warmed, 1-1/2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend, 1/2 cup salsa

Recipe

Place the tortillas on a greased baking sheet. Combine the cheese and salsa; spread over half of each tortilla. Fold over. Broil 4 in. from the heat for 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Cut into wedges. 1 serving: 223 calories, 11g fat , 21g carbohydrate, 9g protein.
